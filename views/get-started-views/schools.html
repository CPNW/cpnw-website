Program Information

CPNW accounts are organized according to degree/program. You must request an account for each degree/program. You must identify an account coordinator and a backup coordinator for each program. Coordinators may manage more than one program. If you don't currently have a backup coordinator, you must specify your primary coordinator as the backup.

CPNW Member vs Community Partner

Certain CPNW Education Packages are exclusively available to CPNW Members. Multi-year Education Packages, in particular, are accessible only to programs with CPNW Membership. Your program's designation is specified in your CPNW Service Agreement.

Multi-year Packages

Multi-year packages are designed for long-term program needs. Review the year-by-year pricing and included services.

First of CPNW's multi-year packages. Year 1 is $185.00/student and includes all onboarding services, OIG/GSA Checks, eLearning modules, and DVS support. Includes 1 background check and 1 WATCH Report. Year 2+ is $55.00/student and includes all onboarding services, OIG/GSA Checks, eLearning modules, and DVS support. Second year also includes a WATCH Report. This is a cost-effective option for long-term program needs who do not require Clinical Placement services.

Second, and most inclusive, of CPNW's multi-year package. Year 1 is $205.00/student and includes all onboarding services, documentation storage, placement services, OIG/GSA Checks, eLearning Modules, and DVS support. Includes 1 background check and 1 WATCH Report. Year 2+ is $80.00/student and includes all onboarding services, placement services, OIG/GSA Checks, eLearning Modules, and DVS support. Includes 1 WATCH Report annually. This is a cost-effective option for long-term program needs, providing program support throughout all aspects of the clinical experience process.
